About Me

I am a passionate educator, empathetic counselor, and creative designer who believes that every person has an extraordinary story waiting to be told — and the potential to flourish.

In the classroom, I build environments where curiosity thrives and no learner is left behind. My teaching philosophy centres on compassion, structure, and igniting a love for lifelong learning across all subjects and grade levels.

As a counselor, I walk alongside students through academic pressure, personal challenges, and career crossroads — offering a safe, non-judgmental space backed by evidence-based guidance.

My creative work spans graphic design, visual communication, and media management. I produce print and digital materials that are not just beautiful — they communicate with purpose and leave lasting impressions.
